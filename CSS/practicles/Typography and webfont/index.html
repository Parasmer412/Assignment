Trends to Watch in 2025 and Beyond

Web development is evolving at an incredible pace. With technologies like AI, WebAssembly, and progressive web apps, the web is becoming faster, smarter, and more accessible.

In 2025, we can expect more frameworks focused on performance and simplicity. Developers are also leaning into accessibility and user-centered design as essential skills.

Whether you're a front-end enthusiast or a back-end expert, staying adaptable is the key to thriving in this dynamic field.
